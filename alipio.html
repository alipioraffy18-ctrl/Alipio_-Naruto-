Naruto Uzumaki

The journey of a boy who went from the village pariah to the Hero of the Hidden Leaf and the Seventh Hokage.

Believe It!

Naruto Uzumaki is a shinobi of Konohagakure (The Hidden Leaf Village) and the protagonist of the legendary anime and manga series created by Masashi Kishimoto.

Born as the jinchūriki of the Nine-Tailed Demon Fox (Kurama), Naruto faced intense isolation and loneliness throughout his childhood. Despite the village shunning him, he set a fierce goal for himself: to become the Hokage so that everyone would finally acknowledge his existence.

Through hard work, an unyielding ninja way ("Never give up on my word"), and an uncanny ability to connect with others, he changed the hearts of friends and enemies alike, eventually saving the entire shinobi world.

Signature Jutsu

Shadow Clone Jutsu

Naruto's signature technique, allowing him to create thousands of solid copies of himself to overwhelm opponents and gather information.

Rasengan

An A-rank technique of pure, spinning chakra passed down from his father, Minato Namikaze, and his master, Jiraiya.

Sage Mode

By balancing natural energy with his internal chakra, Naruto achieves a state that drastically increases his speed, strength, and sensory perception.
